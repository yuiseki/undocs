 The Government of Colombia and the Fuerzas Armadas Revolucionarias de Colombia (FARC-EP) have requested the participation of the United Nations in the international component of the tripartite monitoring and verification mechanism of the Agreement on the ceasefire and bilateral and definitive cessation of hostilities and the abandonment of arms. The mission will be made up of unarmed international observers from member countries of the Community of Latin American and Caribbean States and will have a mandate in keeping with the agreement between the Government of Colombia and FARC-EP. The mission will enjoy full security guarantees and will be requested to begin the necessary preparations for its deployment.
