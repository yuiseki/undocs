 The Security Council reaffirmed its commitment to holding a free, fair and impartial referendum for self-determination of the people of Western Sahara in accordance with the Settlement Plan. It approved the expansion of the United Nations Mission for the Referendum in Western Sahara (MINURSO) and requested the Secretary-General to report by 31 March 1995 on the arrangements for the deployment of MINURSO at full strength, on his plans for implementing the Settlement Plan, and on the responses of the parties. It also decided to consider the possible extension of the mandate of MINURSO after 31 May 1995.
