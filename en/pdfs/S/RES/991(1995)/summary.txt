 The Security Council of the United Nations adopted Resolution 991 (1995) on 28 April 1995, paying tribute to the accomplishments of the United Nations Observer Mission in El Salvador (ONUSAL) and urging the Government of El Salvador, the Frente Farabundo Martí para la Liberación Nacional (FMLN) and all concerned in El Salvador to accelerate the pace of implementation of the Peace Accords. The resolution also calls for continued assistance to the Government and people of El Salvador and affirms that the mandate of ONUSAL will terminate as of 30 April 1995.
