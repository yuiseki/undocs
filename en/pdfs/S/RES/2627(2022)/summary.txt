 The Security Council adopted Resolution 2627 (2022) to extend the mandate of the Panel of Experts, as specified in Resolution 1874 (2009), to 30 April 2023. The Panel is tasked with providing a midterm report to the Committee by 6 September 2022 and a final report to the Council by 3 March 2023. The Council also urges all States, relevant UN bodies, and other interested parties to cooperate with the Committee and the Panel by supplying information on the implementation of the measures imposed by the relevant resolutions.
