 The Security Council of the United Nations adopted Resolution 990 (1995) on 28 April 1995, authorizing the deployment of the United Nations Confidence Restoration Operation in Croatia (UNCRO). The resolution calls upon the Government of the Republic of Croatia and the local Serb authorities to cooperate with UNCRO in the implementation of its mandate, expresses concern that an agreement on the status of forces and other personnel has not yet been signed, and calls on the Government of the Republic of Croatia to conclude such an agreement. The Secretary-General is requested to report to the Council no later than 15 May 1995.
