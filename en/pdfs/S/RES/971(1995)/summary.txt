 The Security Council reaffirmed its commitment to the sovereignty and territorial integrity of the Republic of Georgia and urged the parties to refrain from any unilateral actions that could complicate or hinder the political process. It also called upon the parties to intensify efforts to achieve an early and comprehensive political settlement of the conflict, including on the political status of Abkhazia. The Council extended the mandate of the United Nations Observer Mission in Georgia (UNOMIG) and requested the Secretary-General to report within two months on all aspects of the situation in Abkhazia. It also encouraged the Secretary-General to continue his efforts to achieve a comprehensive political settlement and called upon the parties to comply with their commitments with regard to the return of refugees and displaced persons.
