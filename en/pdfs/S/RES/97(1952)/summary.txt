 The Security Council, in response to a recommendation from the General Assembly, dissolved the Commission for Conventional Armaments. The Council also decided to postpone consideration of the admission of new members until September 2, 1952. This question had also been addressed by the Council in 1946, 1947, 1948, 1949, and 1950. The vote was 8 in favor, 1 against, and 2 abstentions.
