 The Second Committee of the United Nations General Assembly held a general discussion on agenda item 23 (a) and adopted draft resolution A/C.2/77/L.45. The General Assembly reaffirmed its resolution 75/233 on the quadrennial comprehensive policy review of operational activities for development of the United Nations system, as well as its resolution 72/279 on the repositioning of the United Nations development system. It also requested the Secretary-General to present a comprehensive report to the Economic and Social Council covering all provisions and progress made, lessons learned and challenges in the implementation of the mandates contained in General Assembly resolution 75/233.
