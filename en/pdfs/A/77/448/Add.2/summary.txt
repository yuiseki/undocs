 The Second Committee of the United Nations General Assembly held a general discussion on agenda item 23 (b) and adopted draft resolution A/C.2/77/L.62. The resolution reaffirms the UN's commitment to South-South cooperation, calls for the full implementation of the outcome document of the second High-level United Nations Conference on South-South Cooperation, and encourages the continuation and advancement of South-South and triangular cooperation on efforts for COVID-19 response and recovery. It also calls for the development of a framework for the measurement of South-South cooperation, and recognizes the need to strengthen it, including for the use, capacity-building, and transfer of digital technologies.
