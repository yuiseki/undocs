 The General Assembly of the United Nations discussed the Report of the Human Rights Council at its 77th session. The Report included the Council's thirty-second, thirty-third, and thirty-fourth special sessions, as well as its forty-ninth and fiftieth sessions. The Committee adopted the draft resolution A/C.3/77/L.53, sponsored by Ecuador and Liberia (on behalf of the States Members of the United Nations that are members of the Group of African States), with 113 votes in favor, 4 against, and 59 abstentions. The General Assembly took note of the Report and its recommendations.
