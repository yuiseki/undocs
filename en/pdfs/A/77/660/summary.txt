 The Fifth Committee of the United Nations General Assembly discussed the item of "Financing of the International Residual Mechanism for Criminal Tribunals" at its 77th session. The Committee adopted a draft resolution to finance the Mechanism without a vote, which includes the appropriation of 81,945,300 dollars gross (74,951,200 dollars net) for 2023, apportioned among Member States in accordance with the scale of assessments applicable to the regular budget and peacekeeping operations of the United Nations. The resolution also requests the Secretary-General to provide additional and detailed information on the final expenditures and unencumbered balance, and encourages the Mechanism to complete the digitization of its archives.
