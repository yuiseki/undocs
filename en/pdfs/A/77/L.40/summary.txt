 The General Assembly of the United Nations is considering a draft resolution to hold a 2025 United Nations Conference to Support the Implementation of Sustainable Development Goal 14: Conserve and Sustainably Use the Oceans, Seas and Marine Resources for Sustainable Development in France. The resolution would cover the costs of the conference and its preparation, and would involve Governments, civil society, the private sector, the United Nations system and other actors in driving progress and supporting the implementation of Sustainable Development Goal 14.
