 The United Nations General Assembly appointed a Credentials Committee for its seventy-seventh session, which held one meeting on 12 December 2022. The Committee had before it a memorandum from the Secretary-General concerning the credentials of representatives of Member States to the seventy-seventh session of the General Assembly, as well as communications concerning the representation of Myanmar, Afghanistan, and Libya. The Committee recommended the adoption of a draft resolution to the General Assembly, which approved the report of the Credentials Committee.
