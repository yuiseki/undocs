 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/206 on 15 December 2022, condemning the use of mercenaries as a means of violating human rights and impeding the right of peoples to self-determination. It requests all States to exercise utmost vigilance against any kind of recruitment, training, hiring or financing of mercenaries and to consider acceding to or ratifying the International Convention against the Recruitment, Use, Financing and Training of Mercenaries. It also requests the Working Group on the Use of Mercenaries to continue its work with respect to strengthening the international legal framework for the prevention and sanction of the recruitment, use, financing and training of mercenaries.
