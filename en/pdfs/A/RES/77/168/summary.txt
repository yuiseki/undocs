 The United Nations Environment Assembly of the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) reaffirmed its commitment to strengthening the role of UNEP as the leading global environmental authority. It also reaffirmed the Rio Declaration on Environment and Development, Agenda 21, the Paris Agreement, and other relevant major intergovernmental outcomes. The Assembly welcomed the holding of the resumed fifth session of the United Nations Environment Assembly of the United Nations Environment Programme in Nairobi from 28 February to 2 March 2022, and the first special session of the United Nations Environment Assembly for the commemoration of the fiftieth anniversary of the establishment of the United Nations Environment Programme. It also adopted resolutions and reaffirmed the ministerial declaration entitled “Strengthening actions for nature to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals”. The Assembly recognized the devastating global effects of the COVID-19 pandemic and urged support for a sustainable, resilient and inclusive recovery.
