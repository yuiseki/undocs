 The United Nations General Assembly has adopted Resolution 77/267 to review the efficiency of the administrative and financial functioning of the United Nations. The resolution extends the length of the Fifth Committee's session to five weeks, requests the Secretary-General to ensure a strong culture of responsibility and accountability, and reaffirms the programmatic nature of the United Nations budget. It also requests the Secretary-General to assess a procedure to inform the Advisory Committee of possible resource implications, and to ensure effective and efficient utilization of available resources and programme delivery.
