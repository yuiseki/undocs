 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/280 to progress towards an accountability system in the Secretariat. It endorses the conclusions and recommendations of the Advisory Committee, and requests the Secretary-General to continue his efforts to ensure a strong culture of accountability and compliance with regulations and rules. It also calls for the development of an accountability system, including performance management processes, delegation of authority, risk mitigation and safeguard measures, and anti-fraud and anti-corruption frameworks. Lastly, it requests the Secretary-General to ensure that his bulletins are in line with relevant resolutions and decisions of the General Assembly.
