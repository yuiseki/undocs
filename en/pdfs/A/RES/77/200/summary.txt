 The General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/200, taking note of the report of the Human Rights Council, including its addendum and recommendations. This resolution was based on the General Assembly's previous resolutions 60/251, 65/281, 62/219, 63/160, 64/143, 65/195, 66/136, 67/151, 68/144, 69/155, 70/136, 71/174, 72/153, 73/152, 74/132, 75/165, and 76/145. The resolution was adopted at the 54th plenary meeting on 15 December 2022.
