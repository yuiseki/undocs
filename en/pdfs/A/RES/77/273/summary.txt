 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/273, which reaffirms the importance of preventive diplomacy in supporting UN efforts to assist in the peaceful settlement of disputes. It acknowledges the role of the UN Regional Centre for Preventive Diplomacy for Central Asia in assisting the Central Asian countries, and welcomes the efforts of the Regional Centre in strengthening cooperation and engagement of the Central Asian countries on dealing with current challenges in Afghanistan. It also encourages the Regional Centre to continue close cooperation with the Governments of the region to strengthen the capacity of the region to overcome challenges to peace and stability through preventive diplomacy and dialogue.
