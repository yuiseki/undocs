 The General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/231 to follow-up on the Fourteenth United Nations Congress on Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice and to prepare for the Fifteenth United Nations Congress on Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice. The resolution reiterates the invitation to governments to consider the Kyoto Declaration on Advancing Crime Prevention, Criminal Justice and the Rule of Law when formulating legislation and policy directives, welcomes the initiative of the Government of Japan to work with the United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime, and requests the Commission on Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice to continue implementing the appropriate policy and operational measures for the follow-up to the Kyoto Declaration. The resolution also decides to hold the Fifteenth Congress in 2026 and requests the Commission to approve the overall theme, agenda items and topics for the workshops at its thirty-second session.
