 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/162 on 14 December 2022, which promotes sustainable consumption and production patterns for the implementation of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, building on Agenda 21. This resolution recognizes the principles of the Rio Declaration on Environment and Development, the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, and the outcome document of the United Nations Conference on Sustainable Development, and calls for the promotion of sustainable consumption and production patterns in order to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals. It also encourages the implementation of sustainable consumption and production patterns to achieve the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, building on Agenda 21, and urges the international community to support developing countries in strengthening their scientific and technological capacity.
