 The United Nations General Assembly Resolution 77/216, adopted on December 15, 2022, promotes peace as a vital requirement for the full enjoyment of all human rights by all. It reaffirms the obligation of all states to settle international disputes peacefully, to refrain from the threat or use of force, and to promote better relations among all states. It also emphasizes the importance of respecting human rights, the right to self-determination, and the need for a social and international order in which human rights can be fully realized. It further recognizes the importance of peace and development in the prevention of armed conflict.
