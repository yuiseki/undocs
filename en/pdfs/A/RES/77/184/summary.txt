 The United Nations General Assembly Resolution 77/184 reaffirms the commitment to the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development and calls for the full implementation of General Assembly resolutions. It also recognizes the essential role of the United Nations in bringing together a global response to the coronavirus pandemic and calls for adequate, predictable, and sustainable funding of the resident coordinator system. The Resolution requests the Secretary-General to provide timely and publicly available information on the implementation of the mandates in Resolution 75/233 and to present a comprehensive report to the Economic and Social Council and the Assembly.
