 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/215 on 15 December 2022, reaffirming its commitment to promote universal respect for and protection of all human rights and fundamental freedoms. It recognizes the importance of democracy, development, and respect for human rights, and calls for reform of international financial institutions, adequate financing of technology transfer to and capacity-building in developing countries, and policies and measures at the global level that correspond to the needs of developing countries. It also emphasizes the need for respect for cultural diversity and the cultural rights of all, and requests the Secretary-General to bring the resolution to the attention of Member States, UN organs, bodies, and components, intergovernmental organizations, and non-governmental organizations.
