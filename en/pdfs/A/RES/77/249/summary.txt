 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/249 on 30 December 2022, which expresses appreciation to the International Law Commission for its contribution to the codification and progressive development of international law, takes note of the draft articles on prevention and punishment of crimes against humanity, and decides that the Sixth Committee shall resume its session for five days in 2023 and six days in 2024 to exchange views on the draft articles and consider the recommendation of the Commission for the elaboration of a convention. It also invites States to submit written comments and observations on the draft articles and requests the Secretary-General to prepare and circulate a compilation of those comments and observations.
