 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/268 on 18 January 2023, reaffirming the Charter of the United Nations and the right to education, and recalling its previous resolutions and Human Rights Council resolutions on the World Programme for Human Rights Education. The resolution focuses on education for democracy, emphasizing the importance of human rights, the rule of law, and democracy, and calls for the integration of education for democracy, human rights, and sustainable development into education standards and curricula. It also encourages Member States to engage in the Transforming Education Summit process, and invites UN agencies, funds, and programmes to provide resources for the development of educational programmes and materials for democracy.
