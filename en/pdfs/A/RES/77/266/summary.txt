 The General Assembly of the United Nations adopted Resolution 77/266 on 30 December 2022, which established a Working Capital Fund for 2023 in the amount of 250 million US dollars. 150 million dollars will be contributed by Member States according to the scale of assessments adopted for the budget for 2023, and the remaining 100 million dollars will be financed from unspent funds of the 2021 regular budget. The resolution also outlines the credits and advances to be set off against the allocation of advances, as well as the authorization of the Secretary-General to advance from the Working Capital Fund for certain purposes.
