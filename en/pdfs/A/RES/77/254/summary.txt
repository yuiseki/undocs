 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/254 on December 30th, 2022, which outlines the Programme Planning for the Seventy-seventh session. The resolution reaffirms the role of the Committee for Programme and Coordination in verifying that the programmes of activities of the United Nations are implemented in line with the legislative mandates and that the full implementation of regulations and rules is ensured. It also endorses the conclusions and recommendations of the Committee for Programme and Coordination on the proposed programme plan for 2023 and programme performance for 2021, and approves, on an exceptional basis, a programme narrative for programmes 3 and 20 of the proposed programme budget for 2023. The resolution focuses on programme planning for Agenda 2063: The Africa We Want, and is based on the report of the Joint Inspection Unit.
