 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/259, which reaffirms the primary role of the General Assembly in the consideration of reports, the oversight role of the Fifth Committee in administrative and budgetary matters, the independence and separate roles of the internal and external oversight mechanisms, and the important roles of the Office of Internal Oversight Services in contributing to improvements in the effectiveness, transparency and accountability of the Organization. The resolution also requests the Secretary-General to ensure that the annual reports of the Office continue to include a brief description of any impairment of its independence, encourages United Nations internal and external oversight bodies to further enhance the level of cooperation with one another, takes note of the report of the Office on its activities for the period from 1 July 2021 to 30 June 2022, requests the Secretary-General to ensure that all relevant resolutions pertaining to the work of the Office are brought to the attention of the relevant managers, and requests the Secretary-General to ensure that all relevant resolutions, including those of a cross-cutting nature, are brought to the attention of relevant managers and that the Office also takes those resolutions into account in the conduct of its activities. The resolution also notes with appreciation the work of the Independent Audit Advisory Committee and reaffirms the terms of reference of the Committee.
