 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/251 on 30 December 2022, reaffirming the right of all countries to explore and use outer space in accordance with international law, and the need for increased transparency and confidence-building measures in outer space activities. The resolution calls for the implementation of transparency and confidence-building measures to prevent an arms race in space, and requests the Secretary-General to seek the views and proposals of Member States on the practical implementation of these measures.
