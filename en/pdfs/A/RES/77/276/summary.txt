 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/276 on 29 March 2023, requesting an advisory opinion from the International Court of Justice on the obligations of States in respect of climate change. The resolution acknowledges the increasing threat of climate and weather extremes, emphasizes the urgency of scaling up action and support, and requests the International Court of Justice to render an advisory opinion on the obligations of States to protect the climate system and other parts of the environment from anthropogenic emissions of greenhouse gases.
