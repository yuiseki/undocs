 The United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) has adopted Resolution 77/210, which reaffirms the importance of the full and effective implementation of international human rights instruments by States parties. It also recognizes the important role of the human rights treaty bodies in the promotion and protection of human rights and fundamental freedoms, and encourages all stakeholders to continue their efforts for the full implementation of Resolution 68/268. The resolution also requests the Secretary-General to continue to support the organization of discussions on matters related to the implementation of each human rights treaty, and expresses appreciation for the advisory services, capacity-building and technical assistance provided by the Secretary-General.
