 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/159 on 14 December 2022, which emphasizes the essential role of national parliaments in accelerating the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals by 2030. It acknowledges the role and responsibility of parliaments in achieving the Sustainable Development Goals, and encourages Member States to promote engagement and support of parliaments in the follow-up and review processes. It also recognizes the value and contribution of youth to the successful implementation of the 2030 Agenda, encourages communication among parliaments to support sustainable development, and calls upon Member States to promote the leadership role of parliaments in achieving the Sustainable Development Goals.
