 The General Assembly of the United Nations adopted Resolution 77/279 on 18 April 2023, reaffirming the independence of the Joint Inspection Unit and stressing the importance of its oversight functions in improving the governance of the United Nations. The resolution also requests the executive heads of the participating organizations to fully comply with the statutory procedures for consideration of the reports of the Unit, and encourages the Unit to strengthen its outreach and review of long-term effectiveness of its implemented recommendations.
