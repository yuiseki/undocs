 The United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 77/281 on 18 April 2023, which promotes the social and solidarity economy for sustainable development. The resolution recognizes the contribution of the social and solidarity economy to decent work and inclusive and sustainable economies, the promotion of international labour standards, the improvement of the standard of living for all, and social innovation. It also encourages Member States to promote and implement strategies, policies, and programmes to support and enhance the social and solidarity economy, and requests the Secretary-General to prepare a report on the implementation of the resolution.
